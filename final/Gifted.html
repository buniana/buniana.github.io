Gifted

Project Overview

Gifted is a website that enables effective and meaningful gift-search for young adults. This is a class project finished at UC Berkeley over the span of a semester.

Initial Research Question

How do people decide what to give as gifts?

Diving Deep into the Problem Space

- Method: Interview + Observation “Can you show me how you find a gift online?”
- Affinity Diagram
- Based on interview results, we made an affinity diagram and summarized the user needs into key takeaways.
